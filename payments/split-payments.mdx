---
title: "Split Payments"
description: "Split a single payment across multiple recipients or sub-accounts."
---

**Split payments** let you divide a single payment (e.g. from a customer) across multiple recipients or sub-accounts. Use cases include marketplaces (split between platform and seller), gig platforms (split between platform and worker), or internal allocation across accounts.

## How it works

- You create **sub-accounts** or designate beneficiaries that receive a share of incoming payments.
- When a payment is collected (e.g. via DirectPay), you can configure how the amount is split (e.g. by percentage or fixed amount) across those destinations.
- API endpoints support creating sub-accounts and managing split rules. Request/response details are in the [Payments / Payout](/api-reference/money-operations) and related reference (e.g. sub-accounts, payout transactions).

For full parameter and flow details, see [Split payments](/payments/split-payments) and the [API reference](/api-reference/full-api-index).
