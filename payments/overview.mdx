Mono **DirectPay** is the umbrella for Mono’s payment products: **one-time payments** (pay with a link or widget) and **Direct Debit** (recurring collections from a customer’s bank account). Use DirectPay to accept payments, collect subscriptions, or automate payouts.

## What DirectPay includes

| Product                           | What it does                                                                                               | When to use it                                    |
| --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **One-time payments (DirectPay)** | Customer pays once via link or embedded flow. You get instant notification and can confirm status via API. | Single purchases, one-off donations, pay-per-use. |
| **Direct Debit**                  | Customer authorises a mandate; you debit their account on a schedule (variable or fixed amount).           | Subscriptions, instalments, recurring bills.      |
| **Payouts**                       | Send money to bank accounts (disbursements).                                                               | Refunds, earnings payouts, supplier payments.     |

## Why use DirectPay

- **Bank-to-bank** – No reliance on cards; fewer failures from expiry or insufficient funds.
- **Lower cost** – Often lower fees than card-based flows.
- **Recurring** – Direct Debit gives predictable, automated collections with mandate-based consent.
- **One integration** – Use one API surface and dashboard for one-time and recurring flows.

## Getting started

1. [Create an app](/quickstart/create-an-app) and enable Payments to get your **secret key**.
2. For **one-time payments**: follow the [Payment quickstart](/quickstart/payment) and use the [DirectPay API](/api-reference/directpay) (initiate, verify status).
3. For **Direct Debit**: follow the [Payment quickstart](/quickstart/payment) (customers, mandates, debit) and the [Direct Debit guide](/payments/direct-debit-guide); reference the [Direct Debit API](/api-reference/direct-debit).

Configure a [webhook](/utilities/webhooks) to receive payment and mandate events. For full request/response details, see the [API reference](/api-reference/full-api-index).
