**Payouts** let you send money from your Mono settlement account to bank accounts. Use it for salary disbursements, vendor payments, loan disbursements, or refunds. You add a **settlement bank account** in the [Mono Dashboard](https://app.mono.co/apps) so that funds from DirectPay (or other products) can be settled to you; payout endpoints then let you send from that balance to recipients.

## Overview

- **Settlement account** – Where Mono settles your receivables (e.g. from DirectPay). You must add and verify this in the dashboard before payouts.
- **Sub-accounts / beneficiaries** – You can create sub-accounts or designate beneficiaries for split or scheduled payouts.
- **API** – Use the payout and related endpoints to create and track transfers. See the [Money operations / Payout](/api-reference/money-operations) and related API reference for request/response formats.

For step-by-step setup (e.g. adding a settlement account), see [Payouts](/payments/payouts). For Disburse (bulk disbursements from your own account), see [Disburse](/more-products/disburse).
