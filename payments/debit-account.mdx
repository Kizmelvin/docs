---
title: "Debit an Account"
description: "Debit a linked account after mandate is ready."
---

After a mandate is ready-to-debit: optionally call Balance inquiry, then call Debit account with mandate reference and amount. API supports meta and session_id. See [Direct Debit guide](/payments/direct-debit-guide) and [Direct Debit API](/api-reference/direct-debit).
