---
title: "DirectPay Webhook Events"
description: "One-time payment webhook events for DirectPay."
---

Mono sends webhooks for DirectPay. All events include event_id and timestamp. Payment events: authorised, settlement (settled, failed). Fields: flagged, flag_reasons, held_settlement for suspicious or held transactions.

See [Webhooks](/utilities/webhooks) and [Direct Pay integration](/essentials/mono/direct-pay-integration).
