As a credit business, making accurate risk assessment decisions is often challenging. This is because finding reliable ways to retrieve customers’ financial data, determine the maximum amount to lend to them, or assess their capacity to repay loans is hard.

The Mono Connect API product helps you solve these problems by making it easier to access customers’ verified bank statements and better understand their financial behaviour. This empowers you with accurate insights into users’ cash flow and finance patterns, so you can make data-informed lending decisions and lend the right amount of money to the right person.

To use Mono Connect you must be a registered business. [Sign up](https://app.mono.co/signup) and complete the compliance process. Once approved, [create an app](/quickstart/create-an-app) on the dashboard to get your **public** and **secret** keys, then follow the [Connect quickstart](/quickstart/connect) or [Connect integration](/essentials/mono/connect-integration) guide.

- **API reference:** [Initiate account linking](/api-reference/bank-data) and related endpoints.
- **Support:** [Mono Connect articles](https://support.mono.co/en/collections/2631861-mono-businesses-faq) for common questions.

## Features

| Feature                 | Description                                                                | Where to go                                                                                            |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Initialization**      | Start account linking; get a link that opens the Connect widget.           | [Connect quickstart](/quickstart/connect), [Connect integration](/essentials/mono/connect-integration) |

| **Statements**          | User financial statement and transaction history.                          | [Statements API](/api-reference/bank-data)                                                             |
| **Identity**            | KYC: verify user identity, auto-complete account data, reduce fraud.       | [Identity API](/api-reference/bank-data)                                                               |
| **Income**              | Average monthly income, salary estimates, income sources.                  | [Income API](/api-reference/bank-data), [Connect quickstart](/quickstart/connect)                      |

## Connect webhooks

Mono sends events to your [webhook URL](/utilities/webhooks) when something happens on a linked account:

- **mono.events.account_connected** – User linked an account; use the payload to store the account ID.
- **mono.events.account_updated** – Balance/data sync update (e.g. after real-time refresh).
- **mono.events.account_disconnected** – User disconnected the account.

Configure your webhook in the Mono Dashboard and see [Webhooks](/utilities/webhooks) for verification and payload details.
