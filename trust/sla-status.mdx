Mono provides **real-time status** for its APIs and bank connections so you can monitor availability and design for failure.

## Live status

- **Status page:** [status.mono.co](https://status.mono.co) – Current status of Mono APIs, bank connections, and incidents. Use it for operational dashboards and user-facing messaging (e.g. "Bank linking is temporarily unavailable").
- **Dashboard:** Your [Mono Dashboard](https://app.mono.co/apps) and support channels may also communicate planned maintenance or regional issues.

## Building for reliability

- **Webhooks and retries:** If the status page or webhooks indicate an outage, avoid repeatedly calling failing endpoints. Use exponential backoff and honour retry-after where provided.
- **User experience:** When a bank or product is degraded, surface a clear message to users (e.g. "Try again in a few minutes" or "We're experiencing delays with [bank name]") rather than a generic error.
- **Fallbacks:** Where your product depends on a specific bank or endpoint, consider fallback flows or graceful degradation when that dependency is unavailable.

For **coverage** (which banks and institutions are supported), see [Supported Banks & Coverage](/resources/banks). For **encryption and compliance**, see [Data Encryption](/trust/data-encryption) and [Regulatory Compliance](/trust/regulatory-compliance).
