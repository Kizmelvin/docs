---
title: "BVN Lookup (iGree)"
description: "Retrieve BVN details and linked bank accounts with customer consent."
---

BVN Lookup uses the NIBSS iGree consent platform so you can retrieve a customer's BVN details or bank accounts linked to their BVN, with their explicit consent.

## Flow

1. **Initiate** – Start the consent flow; user receives OTP.
2. **Verify OTP** – User submits OTP (or use alternate phone if supported).
3. **Fetch BVN details** – Retrieve identity and/or bank accounts depending on scope.

**Scopes:** e.g. `identity` (includes nationality, address), `bank_accounts`. All requests are billable, including failed lookups; validate BVNs (11-digit numeric) before calling and avoid bulk verification.

For integration steps and request/response details, see [Lookup APIs](/lookup/lookup-apis) and [API reference](/api-reference/lookup).
