---
title: "Account Number Lookup"
description: "Verify bank account numbers and resolve account details."
---

Account number lookup validates and verifies account numbers to reduce errors and wrong transfers. Endpoint: `/v3/lookup/account-number`. Use your **mono-sec-key** for authentication.

For request/response parameters, see [Lookup APIs](/lookup/lookup-apis) and [API reference](/api-reference/lookup).
