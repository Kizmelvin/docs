title: "Lookup APIs"
description: "BVN, NIN, CAC, TIN, address, documents, and mashup verification."
---

Mono provides **Lookup** endpoints to verify identity and business data. Each API accepts the relevant identifier (e.g. BVN, NIN) and returns verification results and related details. Use your **secret key** in the `mono-sec-key` header for all requests.

## Account number

Validate and verify account numbers to reduce errors and wrong transfers.

**API:** [Account number lookup](/api-reference/lookup) | [Docs](/lookup/account-number)

## BVN (Bank Verification Number)

Verify BVN and retrieve linked identity information. Use for KYC and fraud prevention.

**API:** [BVN](/api-reference/lookup), [Initiate](/api-reference/lookup), [Verify OTP](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [BVN i-GREE](/lookup/bvn-igree)

## NIN (National Identification Number)

Verify NIN or vNIN and retrieve identity details. Use for identity verification and compliance.

**API:** [NIN](/api-reference/lookup), [NIN job](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [NIN lookup](/lookup/nin-lookup)

## CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission)

Verify Nigerian company registration details (directors, shareholders, etc.).

**API:** [CAC business](/api-reference/cac), [directors](/api-reference/cac), [shareholder](/api-reference/cac), and related endpoints | **Docs:** [CAC lookup](/more-products/cac-company-search). See also [CAC / Company search](/more-products/cac-company-search).

## TIN (Tax Identification Number)

Verify TIN and obtain tax-related information for individuals or entities.

**API:** [TIN](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [TIN lookup](/lookup/tin-lookup)

## House address

Confirm address validity and house ownership.

**API:** [Address](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [House address](/lookup/address)

## International passport

Validate and retrieve details from international passport documents.

**API:** [Intl passport](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [Intl passport](/lookup/intl-passport)

## Driver's license

Verify driver's license information for onboarding or age verification.

**API:** [Drivers license](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [Drivers license](/lookup/drivers-license)

## Credit history

Retrieve credit history from bureaus (e.g. CRC, First Central) using BVN, with customer consent.

**API:** [Credit history](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [Credit history lookup](/lookup/credit-history)

## 360 view

Combined or aggregated view of identity/account data. See [API](/api-reference/lookup).

## Mashup

Verify NIN, BVN, and date of birth in **one** API call for KYC.

**API:** [Mashup](/api-reference/lookup) | **Docs:** [Mashup](/lookup/mashup)
