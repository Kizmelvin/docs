---
title: "Go Live Checklist"
description: "Required steps before switching to production."
---

Complete the checklist below before you switch your Mono app to **Live** so your launch is smooth and compliant.

## Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Mono Business account">
    If you do not have one, sign up and complete business verification. See the
    [sign-up
    guide](https://support.mono.co/en/articles/4614335-signing-up-for-mono).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit compliance documents">
    Activate live apps by submitting the required documents. See [How to
    activate your business in
    Nigeria](https://support.mono.co/en/articles/7339291-how-to-activate-your-business-in-nigeria)
    or the guide for your region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a dashboard application">
    Create an app in the [Mono Dashboard](https://app.mono.co/apps) to get keys
    for both sandbox and production.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your live webhook URL">
    Set your app webhook URL to your production server so you receive live
    webhook events. Test that your endpoint accepts and processes webhooks
    correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe to a plan (Financial Data)">
    If you use Mono Connect, subscribe to a dashboard plan as required. See [How
    to subscribe to a pricing
    plan](https://support.mono.co/en/articles/7843881-how-to-subscribe-to-a-pricing-plan-on-mono).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add settlement bank account (Payments)">
    If you use DirectPay and receive settlement funds, add your settlement bank
    account in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable two-factor authentication">
    Turn on 2FA for your Mono account so each sign-in requires your password and
    a verification code (email or authenticator app).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch your app to Live">
    In the dashboard, toggle your application from Sandbox to Live when all
    steps above are done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Optional: Post-integration audit

You can request a Post-Integration audit with the Mono team via [integrations@mono.co](mailto:integrations@mono.co) to review your implementation before go-live.

For webhook setup see [Webhooks](/utilities/webhooks). For environments and keys see [Environments and Sandbox](/getting-started/environments-and-sandbox).
