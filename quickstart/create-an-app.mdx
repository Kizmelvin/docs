A Mono dashboard **App** is the foundation for using Mono APIs and products. You create an app to get **public and secret keys** (used in the Connect widget and server-side API calls) and to configure product scopes, webhooks, and branding.

## Before you begin

- You are **not charged** for API calls in sandbox mode. See [Environments and sandbox](/getting-started/environments-and-sandbox) for test credentials.
- There is **no fixed limit** on how many apps you can create.
- To show **Personal** or **Business** banks on the Connect widget, set the Account Type when creating or editing the app.
- To use **DirectPay** (one-time or recurring payments), add **Payments** to your app scope.
- If you **delete** an app, all data tied to that app is permanently deleted.

## Before you create your app

Decide which Mono product this app will use (e.g. **Connect**, **Payments**, **Lookup**). When creating the app you will need to:

- Set an **App name** and **Display name**.
- Add a **branded business logo** (shown to users on the first screen of the Mono Connect widget when they link their bank).
- Choose the **Mono product(s)** the app will serve (e.g. Connect, Lookup).
- Select **scopes** the app should have (e.g. Transactions, Statements, Identity, Accounts, Payments).
- For **Connect**, choose which bank account type to show: Personal, Business, or both.
- Select an **Industry type** for your dashboard app.

For step-by-step guidance on choosing product scopes, see [How to create an app](https://support.mono.co/en/articles/7054553-how-to-create-an-app) in the Help Center.

## Creating your app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Mono dashboard">
    Go to [app.mono.co](https://app.mono.co/). If you do not have an account,
    create one first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Apps and create a new app">
    Click **Apps** in the side menu, then click **Create an app** (top right).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the app details">
    In the modal, enter the app name, display name, logo, product, scopes,
    account type, and industry. Then click **Create app**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use your keys">
    After creation, the app is in **sandbox** mode by default. Test **public**
    and **secret** keys are generated automatically. Use the secret key in API
    requests (e.g. as the `mono-sec-key` header) and the public key in the
    Connect widget.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your app

After the app is created you can:

- **Add or change your business logo.**
- **Add a webhook URL** so Mono can send events (e.g. account connected, payment completed). See [Webhooks](/utilities/webhooks).
- **Switch from sandbox to live** when you are ready for production. See [Go live](/getting-started/go-live).

To update any app setting, open the app, click **Edit**, change the fields, then click **Update app**.

Next, try the [Connect quickstart](/quickstart/connect) or [Payment quickstart](/quickstart/payment) using your new app and keys.
