# Welcome to Mono

Mono is a B2B platform that gives your product access to **linked bank accounts**, **payments** (one-time and recurring), and **identity & business verification** across Nigeria and beyond. One dashboard, one set of keys, and documentation organised by product so you can ship faster.

## Why use Mono?

- **Financial data** — Balances, transactions, statements, income, and enrichment after users link via Connect.
- **Payments** — DirectPay and Direct Debit for collections and payouts.
- **Identity & lookup** — BVN, NIN, TIN, documents, CAC, and more under Lookup.
- **Trust** — Encryption, consent, compliance, and SLA pages for your legal and risk teams.

<Tip>
  **To get started**

- [Sign up](https://app.mono.co/signup) on the Mono Dashboard and complete verification.
- [Create an app](/quickstart/create-an-app) to get your **public** and **secret** keys.
- Configure a [webhook URL](/utilities/webhooks) for real-time events.
- Read [Environments and sandbox](/getting-started/environments-and-sandbox) before you call live APIs.
- Open the [API reference](/api-reference/introduction) when you are ready to integrate endpoints.

</Tip>
