Mono is the open banking infrastructure for Africa. We provide a suite of APIs that allow you to securely connect to user bank accounts, verify identities, and initiate direct payments.

Whether you are building a lending app, a personal finance manager, or a payment gateway, our documentation is designed to help you integrate and scale faster.
