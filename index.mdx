# What is Mono?

Mono is a B2B platform that gives your product access to user bank accounts, payments, and identity verification across Nigeria and beyond. Use our APIs to link accounts, collect one-time or recurring payments, and verify customers and businesses—all with a single integration.

## Why use Mono?

- Link bank accounts and read balances, transactions, and statements in one flow
- Collect one-time or recurring payments directly from bank accounts
- Verify identity (BVN, NIN, documents) and businesses (CAC, TIN) for KYC and compliance
- One dashboard, one set of keys, and clear docs for each product

## How to use Mono

1. **Sign up** at [app.mono.co](https://app.mono.co/signup) and complete verification.
2. [Create an app](/quickstart/create-an-app) to get your API keys.
3. **Pick a product** below and follow its Overview and Get started guides.
4. **Use the API reference** for endpoints, request/response examples, and webhooks.

For sandbox vs live, go-live steps, and FAQs, see the sidebar under **Introduction** and **Getting started**.
