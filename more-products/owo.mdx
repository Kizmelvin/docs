**Owo** is a Mono payment product. When you integrate with Owo, Mono sends webhook events to your configured URL so you can update your system and UI when payments or related actions occur.

## Webhook events

Event payloads and event types are documented in the legacy docs. Configure your webhook URL in the [Mono Dashboard](https://app.mono.co/apps) and verify signatures as described in [Webhooks](/utilities/webhooks).

**Docs:** [Owo webhook events](/more-products/owo-webhooks)

## Security

Always verify webhook signatures using your webhook secret. Do not process unverified payloads. See [Webhooks – Security](/utilities/webhooks).

For product access and integration details, contact Mono or your account manager.
