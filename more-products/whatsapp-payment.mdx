**WhatsApp Payment** lets you collect payments and manage fund requests through WhatsApp. Users can link beneficiaries, request funds, and complete one-time or recurring payments in the chat experience.

![WhatsApp](https://docs.mono.co/icons/product/filled/directpay.svg)

## Overview

- **Fund request** – Request money from customers via WhatsApp; they approve and pay in chat.
- **Beneficiaries** – Link and manage beneficiaries; support one-time and recurring payments.
- **Webhooks** – Receive events for payment and beneficiary lifecycle.

## Getting started

1. Get access to WhatsApp Payment (contact Mono or your account manager).
2. Create an app with WhatsApp Payment enabled and configure your webhook URL.
3. Use the [WhatsApp Payment API](/api-reference/whatsapp-payment) to initiate fund requests, link beneficiaries, and handle payments.
