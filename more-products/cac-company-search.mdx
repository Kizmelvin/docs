CAC APIs let you verify Nigerian registered companies: business profile, directors, shareholders, and more.

## Endpoints

- [Business](/api-reference/cac)
- [Directors](/api-reference/cac)
- [Shareholder](/api-reference/cac)
- [Secretary](/api-reference/cac)
- [Change of name](/api-reference/cac)
- [Previous address](/api-reference/cac)
- [PSC](/api-reference/cac)
- [Status report](/api-reference/cac)
- [Profile](/api-reference/cac)

Use `mono-sec-key` and the identifier required by each endpoint. See [CAC API](/api-reference/cac) and [Lookup APIs](/lookup/lookup-apis).
