**Mono Disburse** lets you send money to many recipients from your own bank account: instant or scheduled, one-off or in bulk. Use it for payroll, loan disbursements, vendor settlements, or cashback.

![Disburse](https://docs.mono.co/icons/product/filled/directpay.svg)

## Why use Disburse

- **Payroll** – Automate salary or contractor payments (monthly or weekly) in one or a few API calls.
- **Loan disbursements** – Credit borrowers as soon as loans are approved; support bulk releases for micro-lenders.
- **Vendor settlements** – Pay suppliers, gig workers, or marketplace sellers on a schedule.
- **Cashback and rewards** – Distribute high-volume, low-value payments for loyalty or refunds.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get access">
    Disburse is available to approved businesses. Create a Disburse app from the [Apps](https://app.mono.co/apps) section after approval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a source account">
    Create a **business source account** linked to a mandate so Mono can debit your account for disbursements. Use the [Create source account](/api-reference/disburse) API, then complete the mandate via the returned activation URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate disbursements">
    Create **instant** disbursements (execute immediately) or **scheduled** disbursements (run at a later time). Support for single and batch disbursements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track status and webhooks">
    Use webhooks and the API to track batch and distribution status (e.g. processing, successful, failed, completed with mix).
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Note:** Disburse is currently available only on the **live** environment (no sandbox).
