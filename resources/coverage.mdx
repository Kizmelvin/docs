Mono provides access to financial data and payments across many institutions in Nigeria and expanding coverage in Kenya, Ghana, and South Africa. For the current list of supported banks and institutions, see [Banks](/resources/banks) and [Mobile network providers](/resources/mobile-network-providers). For API-level coverage (e.g. bank codes), see [API reference](/api-reference/full-api-index).
