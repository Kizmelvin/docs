The **Partners API** lets businesses integrate with Mono Connect with a fully custom experience without the standard Connect widget. You get programmatic control over account linking and can white-label the flow.

## Benefits

- **Full control over UX** – Custom UI/UX instead of the default Mono widget.
- **Programmatic account linking** – Trigger linking via your backend (e.g. SMS, email, or server-side flows).
- **Backend-only implementation** – No embedded widget required; generate links and handle callbacks server-side.
- **Multi-tenant white-labelling** – Different brands or client accounts with a single integration.

## What it covers

1. **Initiate** account linking from your backend (e.g. `POST /v2/accounts/initiate`).
2. **Send** the generated `mono_url` to the user (email, SMS, in-app).
3. **Receive** webhooks when the account is connected and when data is ready.
4. **Call** Financial Data APIs using the returned account ID.
