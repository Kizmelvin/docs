## Other countries

Coverage is expanding in **Kenya**, **Ghana**, and **South Africa**. For the current list of supported institutions in these and other countries, use [Coverage](/resources/coverage).

## How to use this in your product

- **Onboarding** – Restrict bank pickers to supported institutions and countries.
- **GTM & sales** – Reference this page when explaining which banks you can pull financial data from or debit.
- **Risk & operations** – Pair with [SLA & Status](/trust/sla-status) to handle coverage and uptime in your flows.

## Live coverage and status

- **Coverage:** [Coverage](/resources/coverage)
- **Status page:** [status.mono.co](https://status.mono.co)
