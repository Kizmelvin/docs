Use Mono’s official assets on your landing pages and inside your product so users recognize that they are linking their accounts or paying through Mono in a secure way.

## Connect button

Add the **Mono Connect** button to your product to signal that users link their financial accounts through Mono and that their details are safe and secured.

### Button specs (from legacy docs)

- **Default fill**: `#0055BA`
- **Text / icon**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Border radius**: 8px
- **Height**: 55px (example)
- **Width**: 240px (example)
- **Font**: [DM Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans) (recommended)

### Download

You can use the official Mono logo/button asset for “Link Account With Mono”:

- [Download Mono logo (SVG)](https://www.dropbox.com/s/8bllpn2jukuk320/Mono.svg?dl=0)

Use it in line with Mono’s brand guidelines and do not alter the logo or colors in a way that implies endorsement beyond your use of Mono’s services.

## Usage guidelines

- Use the button to initiate Connect or payment flows that are powered by Mono.
- Do not use Mono branding for flows that are not using Mono’s APIs.
- For “Securely connected” or trust badges, refer to any partner-facing brand kit or contact Mono for current assets.
