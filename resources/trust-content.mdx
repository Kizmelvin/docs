Use this page as a source of **trust and security copy** you can show to your end users when they link accounts or pay via Mono.

## Securely connected

You can tell users that:

- **Account linking**: "Your bank account is linked securely through Mono. Mono is an authorised open banking provider. We do not store your bank login credentials."
- **Data use**: "We only access the data you authorise (e.g. balance, transactions) to provide the service you requested. You can revoke access at any time from your Mono or bank settings."
- **Payments**: "Payments are authorised by you and processed via your bank. Mono does not store your card or account details for payment."

## Compliance and security

- Mono is used by regulated businesses and integrates with major banks and payment schemes.
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. See [Data Encryption](/trust/data-encryption) and [Data Privacy](/trust/data-privacy) for details you can share with compliance or legal.
- For NDPR/GDPR alignment and user consent, see [Regulatory Compliance](/trust/regulatory-compliance) and [User Consent](/trust/user-consent).

## Where to use this

- Onboarding screens before the Connect or payment widget.
- Footer or "Security" section of your app.
- Help centre articles explaining how linking and payments work.

Adjust the wording to match your product tone; the above is a starting point.
